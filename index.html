Is your Rig too Big?

Each national park has size restrictions on their RV campsites. The average permitted size is 27 feet, but there are sites that range anywhere from 20 to 40 feet. However there are fewer large sites, so you'll often need to make reservations for them early. Enter your rig size below to find a matching park.
